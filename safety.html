Safety & Security

We prioritize your safety at every step of your online dating journey.

Our Safety Commitment

  • All profiles are verified to ensure authenticity.
  • Private information is never shared without your consent.
  • Our platform encourages responsible communication and respectful interactions.
  • We provide guidelines for safe meetings and online interactions.
  • Suspicious activity is monitored, and immediate support is available.

Tips for Safe Dating

  • Always verify your matches and communicate via our platform initially.
  • Meet in public places and inform a friend or family member.
  • Do not share sensitive personal information too soon.
  • Report any suspicious behavior immediately to our support team.
